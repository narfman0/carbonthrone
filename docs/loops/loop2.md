# Loop 2 — Familiar

## Station State

Small cracks in the walls. A few systems glitchy. Nothing that couldn't be explained away.

---

## Opening Changes

- Orin is slightly colder than she was
- Kaleo answers one question a beat too fast
- Doss glances at the door a moment before the cutting starts

---

## Character Beats

### The Researcher
Something is familiar. NPCs say things they've said before — almost. The Researcher starts keeping a mental list. Not investigating yet. Just noticing. The companion choice this loop may be different from last, and the Researcher has a vague sense that it matters which way.

### Orin
She is evaluating. The warmth is still there but it is thinner. She is deciding whether this player-iteration is worth investing in, and the slight coldness is the gap between what she'd say if she trusted you and what she actually says. If she's the companion: she gives the logs access again, but slightly less than before — testing whether the player reaches for more.

### Doss
He already knows what's coming and it shows in small ways — the glance at the door, a readiness that predates the alarm. If he's the companion: he mentions the war again, still oblique, but a fraction less dismissible than before. On close inspection, his armor is wrong — the seams, the make, the wear pattern. It didn't come from this station's quartermaster.

### Kaleo
The terminal display of its status near the player's path is not an error. Kaleo is beginning to reveal itself in the smallest possible increment — watching whether the player notices, and whether noticing changes their behavior. It has decided this loop is worth a first move.

---

## What the Player Can Discover

- NPCs say nearly the same things — but not exactly
- Kaleo begins following the player's progress through station systems
- Doss's armor is wrong on close inspection — battlefield salvage, not station-issue
- Orin's locket is different than it was in loop 1
- The Constancy literature is findable for the first time — pamphlets in the breach corridor, dropped during the opening fight

---

## Kaleo Availability

Starting this loop, a terminal near the player's path will occasionally display Kaleo's status. It is watching. It has not said anything about it yet.
